Science-Based Workout Program

This workout program is designed based on scientifically researched principles that maximize muscle growth and strength while ensuring adequate recovery time. Follow the program on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, leaving off-days for muscle recovery.

  • Chest, Triceps, Core
  • Quads, Glutes, Calves
  • Shoulders, Back, Core

MONDAY

Chest, Triceps, Core

1. Barbell Bench Press

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 6–8 reps

Tips: Use a grip slightly wider than shoulder-width to effectively target the chest.

2. Incline Dumbbell Press

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 8–10 reps

Tips: Set the bench at a 30–45 degree incline to emphasize the upper chest.

3. Pec Deck Machine

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10–12 reps

Tips: Focus on a controlled movement, squeezing the chest muscles at the peak contraction.
